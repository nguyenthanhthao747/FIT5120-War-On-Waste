Construction & Demolition Waste About Us

We the Cavaliers are an enthusiastic team of Monash students deeply concerned about the War on Waste in Australia. This website elaborates on the existing problem of Construction and Demolition waste in Melbourne region. An in-detailed analysis has gone into - how much waste we have produced, how much we will produce and how we can reduce and recycle.


Keep updated on our latest developments and connect with us to share your own "War on Waste" stories!
